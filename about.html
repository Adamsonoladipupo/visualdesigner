Hello! i'm Abdulwahab

As a UI/UX and brand designer with more than half a decade of experience, I bring a multidisciplinary, human-centered approach and a growth mindset into my design process. My career has taken me through diverse cultures and environments, driving my passion for creating meaningful and innovative solutions.

I've had the privilege of working in various roles, from agency and in-house to consultancy, which has allowed me to empathize with diverse contexts and deliver impactful designs.
Currently, I focus on brand and digital innovation, using my skills to shape compelling experiences. My journey includes pioneering inclusive design practices, establishing digital accessibility and globalization principles, and crafting brands and experiences for a range of clients, from small businesses to startups.

Beyond design, I hold a degree in Physics, which adds a unique perspective to my creative approach. I am an advocate for Computer Digital Literacy, also have a keen interest in systems design, psychological thrillers, cultural podcasts, travel, and crafting unique items. This blend of interests continually fuels my creativity and informs my work as I strive to create designs that resonate deeply with users.
