hello! i'm Ola Adams

I bring a multidisciplinary, human-centered approach and a growth mindset into my design process and craft. Throughout my career, I explored cultures and environments to pursue design - moving to Hong Kong, Seattle and London, and transitioning from agency and in-house to consultancy work. I empathize with diverse contexts to build meaningful and innovative solutions. I currently drive brand & digital innovation at Lippincott, a creative consultancy renowned for making the world's leading brands. Previously, I helped pioneer inclusive design at Airbnb, establishing org-wide digital accessibility and globalization design principles and systems. I created brands and experiences at Branding Records and Rationale and freelanced for small businesses and Y Combinator startups. Outside of design, my evolving interests include systems design (interior, architecture, game, ethnography), psychological thrillers, cultural podcasts and books, indie alternative rock music, travel adventures, homemade recipes, and prints & crafts for my Etsy.
